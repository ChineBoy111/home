Research Interests:

Currently, I am a lecturer of Computer Science at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. I Lead the TExt Analytics and Mining (TEAM) research group at NJUPT. My research interests include text mining, natural language processing, machine learning, especially on Adversarial-neural Topic Modeling and Neural Topic Modeling. Before join in NJUPT, I got my Ph.D degree from PAttern Learning and Mining (PALM) Lab at Southeast University supervised by Prof. Deyu Zhou.
Most of my recent focus is on Neural Topic Modeling (NTM) and Adversarial-neural Topic Modeling (ATM) . Neural Topic Modeling aims to mine semantic coherent topics/patterns from unlabeled texts (such as online reviews and social media posts) via deep generative neural networks or other textual representation learning paradigm. Obviously, Adversarial-neural Topic Modeling (ATM) is a special case of NTM, which is a novel topic modeling framework and mines topics with adversarial training by using the generator network to capture semantic patterns among texts.
